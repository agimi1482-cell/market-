A lively, friendly neighborhood green space

Sustainably-farmed produce, right at your doorstep.

Located in the public garden space at the crossing of Avenue Wannecouter and Rue de Wand, our Friday afternoon market is a vibrant meeting ground for local producers, independent shops, and neighbors.
